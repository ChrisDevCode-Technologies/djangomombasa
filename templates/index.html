About Us

Django Mombasa exists to create a space where developers of all experience levels come together to grow. We believe in peer-to-peer learning — the best way to learn is by building alongside others, asking questions, and teaching what you know.

Our focus started with Django and Python, but our doors are open to anyone passionate about technology. Whether you're writing your first line of code or shipping production applications, there's a seat for you at the table.
